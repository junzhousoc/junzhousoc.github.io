About

Hello! I am Jun Zhou. I am a PhD student in Sociology at University of Michigan. My research interests span the domains of gender relations, international development, political economy, and ethnography. I have earned two MAs in Sociology, and Gender and Development from the University of Chicago and University College London respectively. I have conducted and participated in a range of mixed-methods research on gender and development issues through several research assistantships at universities and NGOs: projects on college women’s employment in Saudi Arabia at Economics Department of University of Chicago, migrant children’s sexual development in Beijing at Beijing Normal University, gender-based violence in South London at Project Oracle, fatherhood and early childhood care in Southeast Asia at Save the Children.
